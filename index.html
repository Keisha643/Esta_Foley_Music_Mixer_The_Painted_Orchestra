How To Play The Music Mixer

When the user clicks and drags the instrument icons from the dock that's stationed in colourful oval shapes surrounding the empty score of the webpage onto the empty score in the center of the main hero image, music will play in a loop.
Multiple sounds can play at once. You can stop, start and reset the audio clips. You can also adjust the volume to your liking.
